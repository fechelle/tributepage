Page Title

Kate Bush

About

Catherine Bush(born 30 July 1958) is an English singer, songwriter, pianist, dancer, and record producer. "Wuthering Heights", her debut single, was on the UK single charts. She was signed to EMI Records after Pink Floyd guitarist David Gilmour . Her song "Running up that Hill" which came out in 1985 is a hit once again this year due to its use on the popular series Stranger Things Season 4. This song has made Kate Bush more known to people globally than just the UK.

To learn more about Kate Bush, see her page at Wikipedia
